Bank-wise Credit Card Count in India

The Indian credit card market has seen tremendous growth over the past few years, fueled by digital transformation, growing consumer spending, and an expanding base of young, tech-savvy users. Credit cards have become a convenient mode of payment, offering a wide range of benefits such as cashback, reward points, and attractive EMIs.

Banks have been pivotal in driving this growth, with several public and private sector banks leading in terms of credit card issuance. This article takes a closer look at the current bank-wise credit card count in India, offering insights into the top banks in this segment and the latest trends.

Growth of Credit Cards in India:

India’s credit card market is expanding rapidly, thanks to rising demand for convenient and secure payment options. According to data from the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), there are more than 100 million credit cards in circulation as of 2023. The number of credit card holders has been increasing steadily with a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 25-30%.

Leading Banks in India by Credit Card Count:

Several banks dominate the Indian credit card market, led by large private sector banks like HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Axis Bank, and SBI Cards. Public sector banks, including the State Bank of India (SBI), also play a significant role in this market.
